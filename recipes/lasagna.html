Lasagna

Traditional Italian Lasagna

The following is a recipe for a traditional homemade Lasagna

Hopefully by the end of it, you'll be able to cook up a tasty treat of your own!

Homemade Lasagna - three parts

There are 3 components to making lasagna

  1. The meat sauce
  2. The white sauce - creamy and thick. but no cream required
  3. Assembling and baking

What goes in lasagna

The meat sauce is basically just like Bolognese

  • Onion, Garlic, Carrot, and Celery
  • Beef
  • Canned tomato and tomato paste
  • Red wine
  • Seasonings - beef boullion cubes, bay leaves, thyme, oregano, Worcestershire sauce

For the white sauce you need:

  • butter
  • flour
  • milk
  • cheese

For assembling, you need:

  • Lasagna sheets
  • cheese!

How to make lasagna

Layering up!

  1. Smear
  2. layer 1: meat sauce white sauce
  3. layer 2: lasagna sheets
  4. repeat
  5. toppings

bake!
