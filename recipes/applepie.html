Apple Pie

The perfect apple pie!

The following is a recipe for a sweet apple pie

Hopefully by the end of it, you'll be able to cook up a tasty treat of your own!

Homemade Apple Pie

There are 2 parts to making an Apple pie

  1. The crust
  2. The filling

The Crust

The crust is basically a shortcrust, for this you will need:

  • Very cold butter
  • Very cold water
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Sugar

To prepare the shortcrust:

  • Pulse in a food processor
  • add the butter in gradually
  • dribble the water in
  • bring the dough together with your hands

The Filling

  • Granny Smith apples
  • Brown sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Ginger
  • Nutmeg
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Egg

Preparing

You will need to prepare the parts to get the perfect apple pie

The filling

Please see below for getting the ingredients prepared for the pie:

  1. Splice the apples
  2. Toss in the spices and sugar
  3. Bake at 180C until tender, not mushy
  4. drain the juices into a saucepan and make syrup
  5. I suggest chilling the filling in the fridge for at least 8 hours

The crust should also be baked with baking beads for 25 minutes at 200C

Now you should fill the pie crust with the filling, and proceed to finish off the pie in the oven!
